Why Foxes?

If I could have any animal as a pet, it would probably be a fox. Foxes are canines that typically hunt rodents. In the winter, foxes walk over the snow listening for rodents moving around underneath them. Once it has pinpointed the sound, the fox will leap into the air and crash down face first through the snow to patch their prey. It is a hilarious sight to behold!
