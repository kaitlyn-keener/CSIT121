Lessons Learned


This project allowed me to see all of the work I have completed over this semester. I think it is important for me to see my growth, and how my knowledge and abilities grew over the past few months. It is a bit of a bizarre feeling to see my websites strung together in a portfolio like this, but also a good feeling, nonetheless. The principles of web designs and standards I have put to use has definitely given me pride.


This course taught me a great deal about coding with HTML, CSS, and other web standards like XML and JavaScript. I built upon my loose foundational knowledge of HTML and CSS that I gained back in high school to make interesting and working websites on my own. I learned what it really takes to build the websites that we peruse every day – which is a lot! – and the effort that goes into making these websites pleasing and accessible. I think next to the understanding of the standards of the web, my biggest takeaway from this course is a newfound appreciation for web developers and their products.
